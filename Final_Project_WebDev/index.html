Reducing Mycophobia: The Fear of Fungi




Mushrooms are a beautiful and essential part of life on this planet, I hope that through exploring this website you will begin to appreciate them as much as I do.

Disclaimer: If you have extreme mycophobia, some photos on this website may distrub you, so be wary as you explore. Below, photos of Clathrus archeri, aka "The Devil's Finger" are displayed which can be found particularly disturbing to individuals with this phobia.




Understanding Mycophobia

"Fungi are Gross"

The Kingdom of Fungi is extremely diverse, and it can be easy to focus on the "creepy" or "gross" appearing specimen, such as Clathrus archeri, pictured to the right.

Admittedly, that is definitely a strange looking mushroom, a friend of mine refers to it as "The Demogorgon Mushroom".
